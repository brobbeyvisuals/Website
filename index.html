About

I make photographs the way other people write letters.

I'm George Brobbey — a photographer based between New York and Accra. I work with people, brands, and publications who care about restraint, quiet, and detail. My pictures lean toward natural light, deliberate composition, and the small gestures most people miss.

Past collaborators include Aesop, Nike, Vogue Italia, The New York Times Magazine, Spotify, and a handful of independent labels and galleries.
